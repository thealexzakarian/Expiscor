About the Project

Project ReGenesis is a cutting-edge initiative aimed at understanding and harnessing the regenerative powers of nature. By studying the Immortal Jellyfish, we aim to develop technologies that can reverse aging and cure degenerative diseases, ultimately improving quality of life for all.

Biological Inspiration

Turritopsis dohrnii, also known as the Immortal Jellyfish, can biologically reset its life cycle by reverting mature cells to a juvenile state. Its unmatched regenerative powers offer a natural roadmap to reverse aging — a perfect subject for cutting-edge biotech research
