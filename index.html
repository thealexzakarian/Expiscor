Biological Inspiration

Turritopsis dohrnii, often called the “Immortal Jellyfish,” possesses the rare ability to biologically reset its life cycle—reverting mature cells back to a juvenile state. This unique regenerative capacity provides an unparalleled blueprint for advancing the frontiers of age reversal and cellular restoration.

...

The Challenge

Contemporary medicine is reactive — addressing symptoms, not root causes. Aging is the silent catalyst behind countless chronic conditions, yet remains largely unaddressed. We lack the tools to repair age-related cellular deterioration at its origin. ReGenesis changes that.

...

Strategic Approach

  • AI-Enhanced Genomic Mapping: Leveraging deep learning to decode the regenerative gene circuits of Turritopsis dohrnii.
  • Cross-Species Translational Bioinformatics: Tracing evolutionary parallels between jellyfish and human gene pathways to uncover therapeutic targets.
  • Predictive Target Discovery: Identifying molecular mechanisms that drive aging and degeneration across tissues and systems.
  • In Silico BioSimulation: Modeling biological interventions virtually to accelerate validation and reduce lab dependency.
...

Objectives

  • Unravel the genomic signature of biological immortality and regenerative capacity.
  • Employ AI to simulate and model age-reversal pathways with clinical potential.
  • Bridge discoveries to age-related diseases including neurodegeneration, fibrosis, and immunosenescence.
  • Establish a digital-first, scalable R&D ecosystem for rapid innovation in regenerative therapeutics.
