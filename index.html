Explore Project ReGenesis

Biological Inspiration

Turritopsis dohrnii, also known as the Immortal Jellyfish, can biologically reset its life cycle by reverting mature cells to a juvenile state. Its unmatched regenerative powers offer a natural roadmap to reverse aging — a perfect subject for cutting-edge biotech research.

The Problem

Modern medicine treats symptoms, not causes. Aging remains the underlying driver of chronic diseases and cellular decline. Humanity lacks the tools to repair age-related damage at the source — until now.

Our Approach

  • AI-Driven Genomic Mapping: Using machine learning to decode regenerative gene networks in Turritopsis dohrnii.
  • Cross-Species Bioinformatics: Identifying human analogs to jellyfish rejuvenation pathways for potential therapeutic relevance.
  • Computational Target Discovery: Pinpointing key genes and molecular mechanisms linked to age-related and degenerative diseases.
  • In Silico Modeling: Simulating biological interventions and cellular responses to accelerate hypothesis generation — without wet lab work.

Goals

  • Decode the rejuvenation genome of the immortal jellyfish to understand natural cellular repair mechanisms.
  • Use AI to identify and model biological processes relevant to aging and regeneration.
  • Apply insights to age-associated diseases such as neurodegeneration, tissue fibrosis, and immune decline.
  • Build a digital-first research pipeline to accelerate discovery in regenerative biology and therapeutic development.

Mission

To lead a global shift in healthcare by turning age-reversal science from theory into scalable, life-enhancing solutions.

Vision

To accelerate the understanding of natural regeneration and resilience through AI-driven research — unlocking new pathways to prevent, delay, or reverse age-related and degenerative diseases.

About ExpiScor

We are a biotech startup merging artificial intelligence, bioinformatics, and regenerative science to build the next generation of longevity therapeutics. Our mission is to decode nature’s most powerful regenerative secrets and make them accessible to all.
