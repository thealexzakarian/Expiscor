Project ReGenesis

Engineering the future of regeneration by decoding nature’s immortal blueprint.

Biological Inspiration

Turritopsis dohrnii, the Immortal Jellyfish, resets its life cycle by reverting cells to a juvenile state, offering a biological blueprint to reverse aging.

The Problem

Modern medicine treats symptoms, not causes. Aging is the root of chronic diseases, but regenerative tools are lacking—until now.

Our Approach

  • AI-Driven Genomic Mapping: Decode regenerative gene networks in Turritopsis dohrnii.
  • Cross-Species Bioinformatics: Map human analogs to rejuvenation pathways.
  • Target Discovery: Identify genes linked to age-related diseases.
  • In Silico Modeling: Simulate biological responses and therapeutic hypotheses.

Goals

  • Decode jellyfish rejuvenation genes.
  • Model aging processes using AI.
  • Apply findings to age-related diseases.
  • Accelerate regenerative discovery pipelines.

Mission

To lead a healthcare transformation by turning theoretical longevity science into real, scalable solutions.

Vision

To unlock resilience through natural regeneration and AI-driven discovery that can delay, prevent, or reverse aging.

About ExpiScor

We are a biotech startup merging artificial intelligence, bioinformatics, and regenerative science to build the next generation of longevity therapeutics. Our mission is to decode nature’s most powerful regenerative secrets and make them accessible to all.
