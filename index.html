Biological Inspiration

Turritopsis dohrnii, the “immortal jellyfish,” can reset its life cycle and revert to a juvenile state. Its cellular regeneration offers a natural blueprint for combating aging — a profound biological phenomenon ripe for exploration.

The Challenge

Today’s healthcare is reactive, not regenerative. Aging silently drives most chronic diseases, yet the tools to reverse its effects remain elusive. ReGenesis confronts aging at its core — with precision, innovation, and purpose.

Our Approach

  • Genomic Mapping: Applying AI to decode the regenerative DNA of Turritopsis dohrnii.
  • Cross-Species Analysis: Identifying human parallels to jellyfish rejuvenation pathways.
  • Target Discovery: Locating critical genes driving aging and disease.
  • Virtual Modeling: Simulating interventions in silico to accelerate research without lab delays.

Our Objectives

  • Decode the regenerative blueprint of the immortal jellyfish.
  • Model aging and repair mechanisms using machine learning.
  • Translate discoveries into therapies for neurodegeneration, immune decline, and fibrosis.
  • Create a digital-first research platform for fast, scalable innovation.

Our Mission

To lead the next evolution in regenerative science by transforming age reversal from theory to therapeutic reality.

Our Vision

To unlock the science of biological resilience — extending vitality, preventing disease, and redefining human longevity through AI-driven research.
